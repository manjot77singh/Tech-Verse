Blockchain

Definition

A decentralized, distributed ledger technology that records transactions across multiple computers so that the record is secure, transparent, and immutable.

Core Features

Decentralization

No central authority controls the network

Immutability

Once recorded, transactions cannot be altered

Transparency

All participants can view the transaction history
